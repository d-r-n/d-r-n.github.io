Bio

I'm an AI practioner and entrepreneur in Austin, Texas. I'm building the tools to support personalized content delivery and platform responsibility in the age of generative AI.

In my day job as a Data Scientist I build and integrate AI solutions to reduce costs, satisfy customers, and drive business success. Currently I'm building an expert assistant for investor relations by finetuning language models with reinforcement learning.

My research interests are diverse, encompassing everything from ranking algorithms and graph technologies to reinforcement learning. I'm always seeking out new challenges and opportunities to further my knowledge and skills.In my free time, I'm working on algorithms that empower users to moderate the content they consume.

The internet is exploding--5M TB today to 180B TB in 2025. Generative AI will further compound this growth as it drives the cost of content creation to zero. I'm building platform solutions for this future. The technology I'm building will allow users to embed their values and interests directly into ranking algorithms. This empowers users to take control over the content they consume. In doing so, this shifts the focal point of repsonsibility concerns from platforms to users, which protects platforms from responsible AI & transparency legislation headwinds.

I believe the right algorithms can revolutionize the way we deliver and consume content, making it more efficient and personal. I'm driven by the potential of technology to change our world for the better, and I'm eager to use my skills to make a difference.
